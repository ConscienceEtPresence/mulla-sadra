Journey I · Existence — al-wujūd

أَصَالَةُ الْوُجُودِ

aṣālat al-wujūd  — assâlat al-woujoud

Primacy of Existence  ·  Primauté de l'existence

Level I — Grasp the notion

In one minute

In one sentence: what makes a thing real is not its essence — what it is — but its act of existing.
Vocalized
أَصَالَةُ الْوُجُودِ
Transliteration
aṣālat al-wujūd
Pronunciation
assâlat al-woujoud
French
primauté (principialité) de l'existence
English
primacy / fundamentality of existence

Fundamental intuition. The word وُجُود names no further thing beside things. It names that by which there is something rather than nothing.

Level II — Understand by experience

The concept does not burn

Take the concept of fire: you can think it, write it, define it — it burns nothing. Now bring a fire that exists: it warms, lights, consumes. Nothing changed in the essence “fire”. What changed everything is existence.

The essence is the same “face” on both sides. Only existence carries from the thought to the effective.
Existence is like light: not one color among others, but that by which every color becomes visible. — The analogy stops there: light is a physical being; existence is not a thing at all.

What it changes: the real stops being a collection of fixed essences to which existence is “added” like a coat. The real is existence — essences are only its profiles cut out by the intellect.

Level III — Follow the reasoning

Ṣadrā's march, step by step

No longer a summarized conclusion — we follow his intelligence.

The question examined

In everything, the mind distinguishes an essence and an existence. Which is the real ground (أَصِيل), which is only a mental consideration (اِعْتِبَارِيّ)?

The terms used

Existence (وُجُود): the act of being. Essence (مَاهِيَّة): the answer to “what is it?”. And one distinguishes the concept of existence from its concrete reality.

The decisive distinction

The essence, in itself, is indifferent: of itself neither existent nor non-existent. It therefore needs a principle that posits it in the real.

The argument from effect

To warm, to light, to cause: no effect proceeds from the conceived essence, but from the thing insofar as it exists. The principle of all effectiveness is therefore existence.

The difficulty he resolves

Objection: “we say the existence of the essence (man exists) — so it would be a second attribute.” Answer: this predication is mental and reverses the real order; in things, it is the essence that is said of an already-determined existence.

The conclusion

“al-wujūd aṣīl wa-l-māhiyya iʿtibāriyya” — existence is fundamental, essence is of the mind. It is the pivot of the whole Ḥikma mutaʿāliya.

The immediate consequence

If the real is existence, and it admits of more and less, the whole of reality becomes a hierarchy of degrees of being. Gradation (تَشْكِيك) opens.

⟡ Indispensable distinctions

  • the mental concept of existence ≠ the reality of existence
  • existence (wujūd) ≠ essence (māhiyya)
  • the unity of the concept ≠ the gradation of the reality
  • absolute existence ≠ determined existences

✕ Common difficulties · risks of misreading

  • Thinking existence is a property added to the thing, like a color. It constitutes it.
  • Thinking all beings have the same degree of existence. It is graded.
  • Thinking gradation is a merely quantitative difference. It is an intensity of being.
  • Thinking the primacy of existence denies essence. Essence remains real as a limit, not as a ground.

◈ Internal questions — and their resolution

How can existence be one and many at once?

By gradation: a single reality, existence, said in degrees. The unity is not that of a genus, but of a modulated reality.

How can a simple reality have degrees?

Because more-and-less is interior to it, like the intensity of one same light — not superadded parts.

How does essence appear, if only existence is real?

Essence is the limit the intellect grasps at each degree of existence: the mental profile of an act of being, not a thing beside it.

Consequence in his system: without this root, neither gradation, nor substantial motion, nor the soul's becoming, nor the return would be intelligible. See Level V.

Editorial work — to be verified, never blindly generated.

al-wujūdu ḥaqīqatun ʿayniyyatun wāḥidatun basīṭatun lā ikhtilāfa bayna afrādihā li-dhātihā illā bi-l-kamāli wa-l-naqṣi wa-l-shiddati wa-l-ḍaʿf
French translation (original)

« L'existence est une réalité concrète, une et simple ; ses individus ne diffèrent pas entre eux par leur essence même, sinon par la perfection et le manque, l'intensité et la faiblesse. »

English translation (original)

“Existence is a single, simple, concrete reality; its instances do not differ from one another in their very essence, save by perfection and deficiency, intensity and weakness.”

Commentary — phrase by phrase

الْوُجُودُ حَقِيقَةٌ عَيْنِيَّةٌ
asserts primacy“Existence is a concrete reality” — ʿayniyya: outside the mind, in things.
وَاحِدَةٌ بَسِيطَةٌ
its unity“one and simple” — not a genus composed of species; a single nature.
لَا اخْتِلَافَ بَيْنَ أَفْرَادِهَا لِذَاتِهَا
the hinge“its instances do not differ by their very essence” — nothing external separates them.
إِلَّا بِالْكَمَالِ وَالنَّقْصِ وَالشِّدَّةِ وَالضَّعْفِ
opens tashkīk“save by perfection and deficiency, intensity and weakness” — the only difference is of degree.
Role of the passage: existence being the real (primacy), the structure of the real becomes a hierarchy of degrees of one same being (gradation). This text is the bridge between the two founding doctrines.

Level V — The notion within the whole system

A root, across the nine parts

Here the notion is no longer a card: we follow it through the Asfār, and watch its function shift according to the subject.

Where it appears · how it is introduced

From the opening of the first journey (Part 1), as the very first acquisition: before unity, cause, or substance. The base that nothing precedes.

How its function shifts by subject

Metaphysics: it founds gradation. Physics: it makes substantial motion thinkable. Theology: God becomes Necessary Existence. Science of the soul: the soul is a degree of existence that rises.

Its role in the unity of the four journeys

It is the thread that makes the itinerary continuous — the same existence, grasped at each stage under a new function.

Journey Iexistence posited as the real
Journey IIit moves and intensifies
Journey IIIit culminates in Necessary Existence
