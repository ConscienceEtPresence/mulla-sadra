The reading key

The four journeys

The work is an itinerary of the soul: a descent into being, then a return. It unfolds once the question is asked.

From creation toward GodExistence, unity, causality — first metaphysics.
In God, by GodMatter, form, motion, time, nature.
From God toward creationDivine science: the Necessary Being, attributes, providence.
In creation, with GodThe science of the soul: imagination, intellect, death, resurrection.
